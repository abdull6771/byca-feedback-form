Babura Youth Care Alliance (BYCA)

Vision: To create a thriving society where young people are equipped with the knowledge, skills, and opportunities to achieve their full potential and contribute meaningfully to our society.

Mission: BYCA's mission is to nurture, support, and inspire youth through innovative programs and initiatives that focus on education, health, entrepreneurship, and leadership development. By prioritizing collaboration and inclusivity, the alliance aims to build resilient communities driven by the active participation of its youth for a better tomorrow.
